## ðŸ”¢ 1. Arithmetic Operators

## ðŸ” 3. Comparison (Relational) Operators

| **Operator** | **Meaning**     | **Example**     |
| :----------- | :-------------- | :----------     |
| **`==`**     | Equal           | **`a == b`**    |

## ðŸ” 4. Logical Operators

| :----------- | :---------------- | :-----------------     |

| **`not`**    | Reverse result    | **`not(a > 5)`**       |
